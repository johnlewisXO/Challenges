Butter Chicken

by John-Paul Lewis

Ingredients

  1. Fresh fillet Chicken(500g)
  2. 1 small onion
  3. 1 small green pepper
  4. Fresh Cream(250ml)
  5. PnP Butter Chicken Cook-In-Sauce
  6. Tomato Paste

Directions

Firstly, put two teaspoons of butter into a frying pan.
Chop up onions and green peppers and put it into the pan.
Cut the chicken fillets into bite size pieces.
Once the onions and green peppers are golden brown,
add the chicken and wait till it is thouroughly cooked.
Next add the fresh cream, cook-in-sauce and tomato paste and stir.
Let it simmer for 10 to 15 minutes, and stir constantly to avoid
it sticking to the pan.
Serve with Rice.



Butter Chicken - Thai Style

This butter chicken recipe is inspired by a popular thai dish, garnished with coriander.



Butter Chicken - Zingy

This butter chicken recipe is inspired by a popular thai dish, made very spicy, as that is the preffered choice for the thai culture.



Butter Chicken - Indian Style

This recipe is made as an authentic indian dish, prepared with boneless chicken.
